Why M365 Profiles?

Why an identity-profile decision tree?

Microsoft 365 ships 30+ SKUs across the Enterprise (E), Business (Bus), Frontline (F), Education (A), Government (G), and Nonprofit families — plus Entra ID tiers, Defender / Purview / Intune suites, Teams Premium, M365 Copilot, M365 E7, and the External ID MAU model. Which one this identity needs depends on who that identity is, not just what features are nice to have. Picking the wrong SKU is a real cost / compliance risk — especially for eligibility-gated tiers.

🎯

One SKU per persona

A privileged admin, a frontline shift worker, a faculty member, and a guest partner all need different Microsoft 365 licenses — sometimes none at all. The right answer changes the per-month cost by 10× or more.

🔒

Eligibility-gated tiers

Frontline (F1 / F3), Education (A1 / A3 / A5), Government (G1 / G3 / G5), and Nonprofit SKUs are validated by Microsoft under the Product Terms. Assigning the wrong tier is an audit / compliance risk, not just a billing one.

📘

Cited to the source

Every recommendation links back to Microsoft Learn, the Microsoft Product Terms, and the m365maps.com visualisations. You can verify every answer in seconds.

What we mean by “profile”. A profile is the identity persona attached to a Microsoft 365 account — not the role, not the device, not the location. The same person can sit in two profiles (a daily-use information-worker account and a separate privileged admin account), and they need different licenses for each. Re-run M365 Profiles once per persona.

Coverage

Profiles supported today

Eight identity profiles plus one warning path. The privileged-admin path is the only one fully built out as a 12-step Yes/No tree — the other seven land on a curated guidance screen with Microsoft Learn and m365maps.com citations. Building those into full sub-trees is the next milestone; contributions welcome.

🛡️ Full tree

Privileged (dedicated) admin

A separate cloud-only identity used only for privileged work — Global Admin, Identity / Security / Compliance / Intune / Teams / Exchange Admin. Entra ID Free until it crosses into PIM, ID Protection, Defender XDR, Purview E5, Intune Suite, Teams Premium, Entra Suite, Copilot, or Agent 365.

⚠️ Warning path

Primary daily-use account with admin roles

Same identity does mail / Teams / Office and privileged work. Microsoft does not recommend this. Always needs a base service license plus any premium tier the role triggers.

💼 Curated guidance

Information / knowledge worker

Office / Teams / SharePoint / OneDrive / Outlook user on a managed PC. M365 E3, M365 E5, M365 Business Premium (≤ 300 seats), + Copilot, + Teams Premium, + Intune Suite, + Entra Suite, M365 E7.

👷 Curated guidance

Frontline worker (F1 / F3)

Shift / deskless / field worker — retail, manufacturing, healthcare, hospitality, first responders, field engineers. Microsoft 365 F1 / F3 at a fraction of E-SKU price. Eligibility-gated under the Product Terms.

🎓 Curated guidance

Education — faculty / student

Qualifying academic institution only. Microsoft 365 A1 / A3 / A5 for faculty and students — different entitlements and pricing per role. A5 Compliance / A5 Security standalone bumps available.

🏛️ Curated guidance

Government — GCC / GCC High / DoD

US sovereign clouds. M365 G1 / G3 / G5 + F-tier Government. Confirm Copilot, Entra Suite, Defender XDR, Purview Premium availability — sovereign clouds lag commercial GA.

💝 Curated guidance

Nonprofit

Charitable org enrolled in Microsoft Nonprofits. Business Premium grant (up to 300 seats), Nonprofit Staff Pricing on Basic / Standard / Premium / E3 / E5 / F3. Eligibility re-validated annually.

🏢 Curated guidance

Small / mid-size business (≤ 300 seats)

Commercial org with no more than 300 seats. Microsoft 365 Business Basic / Standard / Premium. Hard 300-seat cap — above that you must move to Enterprise SKUs.

🔗 Curated guidance

External ID / B2B guest

Partner / vendor / contractor invited as a guest. Billed by Monthly Active Users (MAU) under Entra External ID — not per-seat. First 50 000 MAU free per tenant. Premium features (P1 / P2 / Verified ID) priced per MAU.
